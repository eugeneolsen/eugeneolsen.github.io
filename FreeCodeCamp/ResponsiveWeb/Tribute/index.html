Gordon Bitner Hinckley

A prophet's prophet

President Hinckley moved the Church forward in many ways:

  • He built the Conference Center because the Tabernacle on Temple Square wouldn't hold all who wanted to attend General Conference.
  • He designed small, less expensive temples and built many of them.
  • He reached out to the media and public in an unprecedented way and made the Church leadership more transparent.
